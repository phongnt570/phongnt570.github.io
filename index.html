About

Tuan-Phong Nguyen (Nguyễn Tuấn Phong) is a computer scientist based in Hanoi, Vietnam. His research interests include Large Language Models and Knowledge Bases. He holds a Ph.D. from the Max Planck Institute for Informatics, a M.Sc. from Saarland University, and a B.Sc. from Vietnam National University Hanoi.
